    today's recipe

    spicey meatballs

    Ingredients

    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic
    • 1 teaspoon Diamond Crystal kosher salt
    • 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
    • Â¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper

    method of preparation

    1. Prepare the tomato sauce: In a wide, heavy saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat, about 3 minutes. Add the garlic and cook 1 minute. Add the spices and cook, stirring, 30 seconds. Add the chopped tomatoes. Bring to a boil, then lower heat to medium-low and simmer, covered, 10 minutes, while you're preparing the meatballs.
    2. Prepare the meatballs: In a medium bowl, use your hands to mix the meat and the spices. Divide into 24 equal portions (each weighs about 20 grams) and shape into small, 1-inch meatballs.
    3. Add the meatballs to the tomato sauce. Turn heat to medium-high and bring to a boil, then lower heat back to medium-low, cover and simmer for 30 minutes. Sprinkle with the cilantro and serve.

    Additional Information

    Tasty and versatile, they can be prepared with different seasonings, accompany the tomato sauce or even serve as a snack when made in smaller versions to receive visitors.
